# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Clutch is built around neighbors helping neighbors. We hold contributors to the same standard.

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, background, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

---

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**

- Be respectful and considerate in all communication
- Welcome newcomers and help them get oriented
- Give and receive constructive feedback gracefully
- Focus on the work, not the person

**Unacceptable behavior:**

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks of any kind
- Trolling or deliberately inflammatory comments
- Publishing someone's private information without consent
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this standard. Reports of unacceptable behavior may be sent to the project owner. All reports will be reviewed promptly and kept confidential.

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject any contribution that violates this Code of Conduct, and may ban contributors who engage in persistent or severe violations.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces — GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other official project channel.
